# Source Priorities

Search widely and do not stop at the first usable text.

Strongly prefer:

- The Dawn-Breakers
- Memorials of the Faithful
- Bahá'í Reference Library
- Star of the West
- Promulgation of Universal Peace
- Paris Talks
- God Passes By
- The Priceless Pearl
- The Covenant of Bahá'u'lláh
- Bahá'í World
- memoirs, biographies, recollections, and pilgrim notes with clear attribution
- respected Bahá'í story collections

Avoid repeatedly using the same source family on consecutive runs unless the story is clearly extraordinary.

For The Dawn-Breakers, choose only dramatic, sacrificial, heroic, emotionally powerful, or historically unforgettable passages. Avoid merely logistical or chronological sections.
