# Image Rules

- The single new daily story becomes the featured story for that day unless a stronger same-day replacement is found during rerun.
- Use one tasteful, high-quality featured image only.
- Prefer Bahá'í Houses of Worship, historic Bahá'í sites, authentic Bahá'í archival photos, 'Abdu'l-Bahá, early believers, or historic gatherings when directly relevant.
- Use tasteful symbolic imagery only when a direct image is unavailable.
- Reject weak generic architecture, unrelated scenery, repeated recent images, low-quality images, or image/story mismatches.
- The featured image must load successfully.
- The featured image should not repeat the previous featured image and should not appear on non-featured cards.
- Non-featured cards remain text-first unless intentionally designed otherwise.
