About

A quiet reader for stories with roots.

Clara's Stories is being shaped as a calm, source-conscious way to read Bahá'í stories, letters, and passages without losing the thread of where they came from.

Next room

Letters need a different kind of attention.

The Universal House of Justice's letters should not be dropped into the same stream as stories. They need their own reader: date, subject, quotation, context, and a way to return to guidance over time.
